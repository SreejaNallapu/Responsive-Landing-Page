Our Story

Built by architects who wanted to slow the process down.

Studio Lumen started in 2014 out of a small studio in Banjara Hills with a simple frustration: too many projects rushed straight to elevations without understanding how a family or a team actually moved through a day. We built our process around that idea instead — light studies, material sampling on-site, and honest conversations about budget before a single drawing is finalised.

  • Site-first design process, tailored to climate and context
  • In-house 3D visualisation before a single wall is built
  • Transparent budgeting with vetted local contractors
  • Post-handover support for the first year of living in the space

How We Work

A process built on three commitments

Every engagement, big or small, moves through the same disciplined stages.

Listen & Study

We start with the site, the brief and the people using the space — before any concept sketch.

Model & Refine

3D models and material boards let clients experience the space long before construction begins.

Build & Support

We stay on site through execution and remain reachable for a full year after handover.
