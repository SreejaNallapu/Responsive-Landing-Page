Who We Are

Considered design, built for how you actually live.

Founded in 2014, Studio Lumen is a Hyderabad-based architecture and interior design practice. We work across residential, commercial and hospitality projects — always starting from the way a space will be used, not just how it will photograph.

  • Site-first design process, tailored to climate and context
  • In-house 3D visualisation before a single wall is built
  • Transparent budgeting with vetted local contractors
